# Privacy Policy for Eigen Bot

**Last Updated: January 4, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Eigen Bot ("the Bot", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Discord bot services.

By using Eigen Bot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Bot, we automatically collect:
- **Discord User ID**: Your unique Discord identifier
- **Server/Guild ID**: The ID of Discord servers where the Bot is installed
- **Channel IDs**: IDs of channels where the Bot operates
- **Message Content**: Only messages that directly interact with the Bot's commands
- **Usage Data**: Command usage, timestamps, and interaction patterns

### 2.2 Quiz and Leaderboard Data

We collect and store:
- Your quiz scores and statistics
- Your current and best streaks
- Your ranking on leaderboards
- Daily quest completion progress
- Reward inventory (streak freezes, bonus hints)

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do **NOT** collect:
- Your Discord password or authentication tokens
- Private messages not directed to the Bot
- Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
- Payment or financial information
- Location data
- Device information or IP addresses

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain Bot functionality
- Track quiz scores and leaderboard rankings
- Manage daily quests and reward systems
- Improve user experience and Bot features
- Monitor and prevent abuse or misuse
- Generate usage statistics and analytics

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Storage
- Data is stored in a secure SQLite database
- All data is stored locally on our servers
- We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data

### 4.2 Security Measures
- Database access is restricted and protected
- We use industry-standard security practices
- Regular backups are performed to prevent data loss

### 4.3 Data Retention
- User data is retained as long as you actively use the Bot
- Inactive user data may be archived or deleted after extended periods
- You can request data deletion at any time (see Section 8)

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 We Do NOT Share Your Data

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your personal information with advertisers
- Provide your data to data brokers
- Use your data for targeted advertising

### 5.2 Limited Disclosure

We may disclose information only when:
- Required by law or legal process
- Necessary to protect our rights or property
- Necessary to prevent harm or illegal activity
- With your explicit consent

## 6. Discord's Privacy Policy

As a Discord bot, we operate within Discord's platform. Discord's own Privacy Policy applies to all interactions on their platform. We encourage you to review Discord's Privacy Policy at: https://discord.com/privacy

## 7. Children's Privacy

The Bot is intended for users aged 13 and older, in compliance with Discord's Terms of Service. We do not knowingly collect information from users under 13. If we discover that a user under 13 has provided information, we will delete it promptly.

## 8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

### 8.1 Access Your Data
Request a copy of all data we have stored about you.

### 8.2 Delete Your Data
Request deletion of your data at any time. This will:
- Remove your quiz scores and statistics
- Remove your leaderboard entries
- Delete your daily quest progress
- Erase all associated user data

### 8.3 Opt-Out
You can stop using the Bot at any time by:
- Removing the Bot from your server (server owners)
- Not interacting with the Bot's commands

### 8.4 How to Exercise Your Rights
To exercise any of these rights, contact us through:
- GitHub: https://github.com/youngcoder45/Eigen-bot-In-Python/issues
- Discord Support Server: [Your support server invite link]

## 9. Cookies and Tracking

The Bot does not use cookies or tracking technologies. We only collect data through direct interactions with Bot commands.

## 10. Third-Party Services
